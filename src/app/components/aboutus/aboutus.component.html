About Project

The project idea revolves around creating a luxury cinema on board a yacht moving along the shores of Porto Marina. Passengers can enjoy high-quality cinema screenings and luxury in an atmosphere of nature and adventure.

Objective

Unique Experience: Providing a luxurious and unique cinema experience, blending the concept of a sea voyage with cinematic entertainment.

Luxury and Privacy: Offering a unique experience for customers who wish to enjoy cinema in a private and luxurious setting.

Explore Natural Landscapes: Allowing the audience to view unique and beautiful natural scenery while watching movies.

Features:

Comfortable and Luxurious Venue: The yacht is equipped with a luxurious cinema hall with comfortable seats and high-quality sound and picture systems.

Food and Beverages: Providing complementary entertainment services, such as offering light meals and luxury drinks to passengers while watching.

Nile Cruise: An opportunity to enjoy a wonderful Nile cruise while watching movies, creating an exceptional experience.

Sustainability:

Eco-Friendly Technology Using clean energy technology and eco-systems to keep the beaches clean.

Promoting Tourism: Eco-Friendly Technology Contributing to enhancing environmental and cultural tourism in the region. Financial Sustainability:

Carefully Set Pricing: Setting reasonable and inspiring prices to be affordable for everyone.


A cinema project on a yacht along the shores of Porto Marina can stimulate tourism and offer a luxurious and unique entertainment experience while ensuring environmental and economic sustainability.
